Radhe Krishna Gaushala located in Pali, Rajasthan has been developed in a green envirnment and currently a house over 500 cows.The main moto and purpose of the Gaushala is to protect and look after every needy cow.
Our Vedas say that Mātaraha sarvabhūtanām gāvaha, the cow is the mother of mothers since it has the ability to feed, provide and support humanity in many ways.

Leader of Radhe Krishna Gaushala says,"जो मनुष्य प्रतिदीन स्नान करके गौ का स्पर्श करता है,वह सब पापों से मुक्त हो जाता है। जो गौओं के खुर से उडती हुई धूली को सिर पर धारण करता है,वह मानों तीर्थ के जल में स्नान कर लेता है और सभी पापों से छुटकारा पा जाता है।
Another important thing of a cow is its urine(gau-mutra) and gau mai (cow dung) have medicinal uses in Ayurveda,Organic Farming and used for purification in sacred rituals.
Best cow food is given on time to every cow and nutrition check is done every week by the respective doctors.
From the best food provided to them pure products are made like milk , yogurt,ghee(refined Butter), cow dung and gau mutra.

In lots of spiritual "yagyas", the fire is burnt using dried cow dung and ghee. It is said that burning Cow dung with ghee is one of the best way to purify the home.
Well scientifically, researchers have found out that the cow dung purifies the air and also has anti-pollution and anti-radiation qualities in the environment.
By having fresh cow milk we can increase our immunity system,Intake of cow urine helps recover from cancer and Tuberculosis(t.b).

In other countries like Europe and Switzerland practice is done of cow cuddling which is also known as "cow hugging" in which people spend time with cows ,Cow cuddling is believed to promote positivity and reduce stress by boosting oxytocin in humans.
Use of cow dung can reduce the price of petrol cause cow-dung by some process can turn into a useful gas.

There are many importance of cow wastes,That's why in Indian ritual cow is called Kamdhenu(gau-mata)
